# About

**María Isabel Marín Morales** (Pepo)

PhD candidate at the **University of Groningen**, Netherlands. My research sits at the intersection of computational linguistics, cultural studies, cognitive linguistics, and AI.

## Research focus

My doctoral work analyzes conceptual metaphors in Colombia's Truth Commission (CEV) Final Report, examining how metaphors structure narratives around armed conflict, peace, and environmental sustainability.

## Background

- Systems and Informatics Engineer · Universidad Nacional de Colombia
- Former university professor at Universidad de Antioquia and Tecnológico de Antioquia
- Former analyst at Colombia's Truth Commission (CEV)
- Research stay at Harvard SEAS
- Team member on European Digital Humanities federated platform project

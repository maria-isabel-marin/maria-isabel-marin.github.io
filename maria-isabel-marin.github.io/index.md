# María Isabel Marín Morales

Researcher at the intersection of **computational linguistics**, **cultural studies**, and **AI**. PhD candidate at the University of Groningen.

I work on how language — especially metaphor — shapes narratives around conflict, peace, and territory. My doctoral research analyzes conceptual metaphors in Colombia's Truth Commission Final Report.

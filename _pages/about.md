I am a researcher at the intersection of **computational linguistics**, **cultural studies**, and **AI**, currently pursuing my PhD at the University of Groningen (Netherlands).

My doctoral research analyzes conceptual metaphors in Colombia's Truth Commission (CEV) Final Report, examining how language structures narratives around armed conflict, peace, and environmental sustainability. I combine corpus linguistics, cognitive linguistics, and NLP methods to study metaphor at scale.

**Research interests:** Conceptual Metaphor Theory · NLP for Spanish · Digital Humanities · Peace & Conflict Studies · Corpus Linguistics

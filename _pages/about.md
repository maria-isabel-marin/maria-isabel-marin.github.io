I am a Systems & Informatics Engineer, Master of Science from the Universidad Nacional de Colombia, and PhD candidate in the Graduate School of Humanities at the University of Groningen (Netherlands). My doctoral research — funded by Colombia's Ministry of Science, Technology and Innovation (Minciencias, Call 906/2021) — develops a computational framework for analyzing cultural discourse through conceptual metaphor, under the title *From metaphor to narrative: Computational modelling of cultural discourse through MELT and Large Language Models*.

My work sits at the intersection of **computational linguistics**, **cognitive linguistics**, **NLP**, and **digital humanities**, with a focus on Spanish-language corpora and conflict/peace narratives. I combine corpus methods, metaphor theory, and AI to study meaning at scale.

I also bring hands-on experience across much of the software development lifecycle — from requirements elicitation and functional specification to back-end and front-end implementation, with a particular emphasis on **data management**: database design and ETL pipelines. As a software developer I have contributed to interdisciplinary projects in the areas of water resource management, applied linguistics, and transitional justice. This technical background coexists with my work as a university lecturer and researcher, shaping a hybrid profile that bridges **software engineering**, **teaching**, and **computational humanities**.

**Research interests:** Conceptual Metaphor Theory · NLP for Spanish · Digital Humanities · Peace & Conflict Discourse · Corpus Linguistics · Large Language Models

---

**Current projects**

- **MetaphorModel.org** — AI-powered computational ecosystem for cultural narrative analysis, comprising:
  - *Metaphor Manager* — management and analysis of metaphorical expressions in textual corpora
  - *MELT Processing* — automatic metaphor detection via Metaphor Field-Loop Theory
  - *Research Corpus* — exploration platform for the conceptual metaphor corpus extracted from Colombia's Truth Commission Final Report
- **PhD thesis** — *From metaphor to narrative*, supervised by Dr. Pablo Valdivia and Dr. Marc Esteve del Valle (University of Groningen)
